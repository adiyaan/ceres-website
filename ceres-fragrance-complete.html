Products and Services

Product Intro

We sell high-quality fragrances designed to capture the essence of the outdoors. Each fragrance is crafted using natural ingredients that highlight different aspects of nature, from lush forests to vibrant wildflowers. Our scents incorporate elements of both flora and fauna to create an immersive connection to the natural world. We will express their unique beauty and connection to some aspect of nature through names relating to Greek mythology and each aspect of it relating to the creation of the earth and its species.

Features

Fragrance: Our products use natural base ingredients and direct oils/accords to create strong yet subtle scents. Designed for long-lasting wear, the formulas balance scent strength, skin safety, and harmony between top, middle, and base notes.

Bottles: Each fragrance is housed in a medium-thick glass bottle, overlaid with decorative wood, offering visual appeal and brand consistency. Travel-size aluminum versions are offered for portability. Application varies between atomizers and dab-on styles.

Product Mix: Fragrances are created using nature-based notes like woods, florals, musks, and vanilla. We use accords to blend these into entirely new scent experiences that still feel grounded in the natural world.

Product Safety: We carefully avoid irritants and use safe concentrations. Ingredients are natural but tested to ensure skin-friendliness.

Lasting Scent: Our fragrances are structured with top, mid (heart), and base notes to last throughout the day, adapting as you move.

What Need Does the Product Meet?

Many young men and athletes struggle to find a long-lasting, non-irritating, fresh scent. Our fragrances solve this with natural, skin-safe ingredients that blend with active lifestyles and still feel fresh and confident all day long.

What Is Unique?

We focus on outdoor-appropriate, sweat-adaptive fragrances that use nature-derived ingredients, designed to enhance rather than overpower, and to last comfortably on the skin.

Competitive Advantage

Our organic formulas, recyclable packaging, and mythology-inspired themes make our brand stand out. Partnerships and potential patents will protect our uniqueness while we build a loyal customer base who loves both nature and fragrance.
